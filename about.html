Junglefowl BJJ is located at 86 Main Street in downtown Sackville, New Brunswick — a renovated basement facility with a entrance to the left of the building, in the back.

The gym features a 1500 sq ft matted area with tatami mats, wall mats, a half cage, and heavy bags — plus a strength and conditioning area and a children's play area with slides and climbing ropes.

We run a clean, safe, family-friendly environment focused on a healthy lifestyle. See our media section or the schedule for class offerings.
