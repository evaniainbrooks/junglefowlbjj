Our journey started in Port Elgin, New Brunswick just before the pandemic. Over the years we grew our programs, honed our skills, and it became apparent that the next evolution would be to relocate the gym to serve a larger client base.

We are proud to announce that our newly renovated (February 2024) training facility is located at 86 Main Street in downtown Sackville, New Brunswick. The entrance to the basement facility can be found to the left of the building, in the back. Just follow the signs.

The main gym facility consists of a 1500 square foot matted area, outfitted with brand new tatami mats, perfect for any kind of martial arts training. This area is lined with wall mats and a half cage, creating a realistic MMA training experience. Heavy bags, banana bags and other striking equipment can also be found along the perimeter.

Next to the main training area, on other side of the cage is our strength and conditioning area, outfitted with all of the equipment one would expect to find in a CrossFit or weightlifting gym.

Finally, we have an area dedicated to the children. Here we have a large play structure with slides, climbing ropes, and other equipment geared towards the little ones.

We pride ourselves on providing a clean, safe, family friendly training environment. Our primary goal is to promote a healthy lifestyle, and have fun! There is truly something for everyone at Junglefowl. Check out our media section to learn more about our facility.

To see our weekly group class schedule and learn more about specific programs offered, see our schedule page.

  • Q:Do you offer a free trial?

    A:Yes, you can try all of our classes for an entire week at no charge.

  • Q:What is the cost?

    A:We have a straightforward pricing structure, everything can be found on our prices page. We don't force you to sign any contract.

  • Q:What ages are the kids class?

    A:We offer kids class from ages 5-15, though sometimes younger children are able to follow along and participate. It all depends on the individual. Older children are also encouraged to join the adult classes.

  • Q:Do you offer any discounts?

    A:Yes, we offer status discounts (student/military/other), as well as membership savings when you pay up front. It's all listed on our prices page.

  • Q:What should I expect on my first day?

    A:Try to show up 10 minutes early so you can sign a waiver and speak with the instructor. Wear comfortable athletic clothing, preferably with minimal zippers, buttons, or other metal parts. The only other things you might need are a water bottle and a positive attitude!

  • Q:How can I pay?

    A:We accept cash or card at the gym, or e-transfer to payments@junglefowlbjj.ca

  • Q:I am a personal trainer/group fitness instructor. Can I rent your space to hold my classes?

    A:Yes, just send us a message and we will find a spot in our group fitness schedule. With 1600+ square feet of mat area, we can accommodate multiple classes at the same time.

  • Q:Do you offer private lessons?

    A:Yes, private lessons start at $35 per hour + HST. Sessions typically run closer to 90 minutes in order to provide top value. Check out our instructors page to learn more about us.

  • Q:What is your affiliation?

    A:None. We are an independent gym.
